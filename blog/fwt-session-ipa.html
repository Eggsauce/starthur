Our Certificates

Halal

Our products are certified Halal by the Department of Islamic Development Malaysia (JAKIM). Our products do not contain any substance that is considered impure in Islamic law.


MeSTI

Suree Food adopts MeSTI – a food safety program by the Malaysian Ministry of Health to put in place a system for the maintenance of food hygiene and process control. This includes food safety assurance and food traceability.
