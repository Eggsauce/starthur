Welcome to

Suree Food

Suree Food is a Malaysian-based family business with more than 40 years’ experience in producing quality fish sauce since 1976. Our company located in Sungai Petani, Kedah and gradually has grown over the years and distributes locally throughout all Malaysia regions.

Today, we are one of the most reputable fish sauce manufacturers in the market by offering our home brand fish sauces, raw fish sauce, OEM and wholesale services. We use the best ingredients sourced to guarantee that our customers’ products are all top quality before it is released into the market or sold to their clients.

Also, our employees are trained intensively, with a huge emphasis on hygiene and good manufacturing practice in every stage of our manufacturing process. Our family formed the company to market what has now become a staple sauce, seasoning and condiment in various cuisine.

  • 1972

    Humble Beginning

    Ch'ng family started business in a small town located in Sungai Batu Bujang, Kedah. At first, we only selling fish sauce in drum.

  • 1996

    Adapting to Market Changes

    Due to the increase in household demand for fish sauce, we start to introduce fish sauce in bottle packaging.

  • 2002

    Moving

    We were able to move to a new and bigger factory located in Sungai Tukang, Kedah. We purchased a semi-automatic machine to support large orders from our customers.

  • 2005

    Growth

    We purchased new partly automated machine to improve the production performance.

  • 2015

    Expansion

    We purchased a new fully automated packaging machine. Business expansion to new market and regional.

  • 2021

    Where We Are Today

    Today, we are one of the most reputable fish sauce manufacturers in the market. Our business has grown over the years and distributes locally throughout all Malaysia regions.
